Hi! I am a
Front-End Developer,
and my name is Derya

My coding journey started at the end of 2018 with Java. After several months of tutorials and practice, I familiarized myself with language basics and decided to give Javascript a go, thus paving the way for my front-end development path. Then, I realized that I am passionate about design, centering divs, memorizing variable names, googling bugs, rendering my page on Internet Explorer, even reading technical documents. Front-end is my passion, and I am a front-end monster.

I also enjoy photography, cooking, human languages, retro design of 90s, and Tolkien’s Middle Earth. I hold a bachelor’s degree from American Culture and Literature. Previously I had roles in Sales and Logistics consecutively. I can communicate in Turkish, English, Italian as well as Russian (a bit).

Currently, I am working on my next front-end project. I share my coding journey through #100DaysOfCode, sometimes publish articles on DEV.to, sketch on CodePen, and mostly find myself reading about various topics ranging from Computer Science, programming, and Javascript.

Welcome to my first website, and stay connected for my next projects!
